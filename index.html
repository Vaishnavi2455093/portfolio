HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

Tools & Skills Used

Power BI (Dashboard Development, Data Visualization) DAX (KPI Measures, Calculations) Data Cleaning & Transformation Data Modeling

Project Description

Developed a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, satisfaction, and workforce performance across 1,480 employees.

Tracked six core KPIs including a 16.1% attrition rate, average job satisfaction of 2.73/5, and average tenure of 7.01 years.

Identified Life Sciences and Medical as highest attrition fields, with R&D contributing 133 of 238 total departures.

Analyzed overtime impact — employees with overtime showed lower satisfaction scores and higher likelihood of leaving the organization.

Revealed 80%+ of workforce falls in below-10K salary bands, highlighting compensation as a key lever for retention strategy.

Built using Power BI with DAX measures, interactive slicers for Department and Gender, and thorough data cleaning and modeling.

Credit Card Financial Analysis

Tools & Skills Used

Power BI (Dashboard Development, Data Visualization) DAX (KPI Measures, Calculations) Data Cleaning & Transformation Data Modeling

Project Description

Designed and developed an end-to-end Credit Card Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to analyze customer behavior, transaction patterns, and revenue performance across 32K+ customers.

Performed customer segmentation by income group, education level, marital status, age, gender, and job role to identify high-value segments.

Analyzed quarterly revenue and transaction trends — Q4 identified as peak activity period with highest revenue and transaction volume.

Revealed swipe-based transactions as the dominant payment method, with Bills and Entertainment as top spending categories.

Evaluated card category performance — Blue and Silver cards showed higher usage compared to Gold and Platinum tiers.

Enabled stakeholders to identify revenue drivers, optimize product offerings, and support data-driven marketing strategies.
